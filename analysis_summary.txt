Netflix Data Analysis - Summary of Findings
----------------------------------------------------
This file summarizes the insights obtained after running SQL analysis queries.

Key Insights (example):
1. Movies make up around 70% of Netflix's total content.
2. The United States and India are top content-producing countries.
3. 'Dramas' and 'International Movies' are the most frequent genres.
4. The number of titles added has increased sharply after 2015.
5. Most Netflix titles are rated for mature audiences (TV-MA).
